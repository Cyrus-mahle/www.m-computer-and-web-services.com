About Me

Nogaya Sandiso is a visual artist who works with paint and printmaiking Born 5th of march 1999 at Tabankulu, Eastern Cape Province,He is currently living and studying at East london He is doing Diploma in fine at Walter Sisulu University majoring with paint and printmaiking since 2020, His journey started long time ago , He has worked with local fine artist such as Mzukisi Luvela,Esethu Mguzumbane and Luvo Guzane at Tabankulu art center

We can checkout his recent works and experiences below. He considers art as his passion more than profession.

Artist Statement

  • TAKING A LEAF OUT OF A SUCCESSFUL MAN'S BOOK
    My inspiration for creating art comes from hardworking individuals. My artworks are a reflection of the daily inspirations I experience, particularly the drive to work hard to put food on the table despite a difficult upbringing. My artwork is built on inspiring people and providing them with a sense of hope that will keep them going and keep them striving for more. My pieces of art entice the audience to do more investigation and learn more because they raise questions and contain symbolism. My audience consists of both those who have overcome numerous obstacles and have worked hard to get where they are and those who are still in school as a means of surviving and succeeding. My artwork expands more on my discoveries as an artist and proves that hard work pays off. My artwork should, in my expectation and hope, inspire amazement, joy, excitement, motivation, and cheerfulness.
  • As the contemporary artist, I look up on other contemporary artists to better understand and apply some techniques. on printmaking and painting there is variation of symbols. On printmaking I used woodblocks and on painting, self stretched canvases with rough and smooth primed texture to commemorate the viewer to the walked journey up to this far. They create thinking and imagination time. I am inspired by many artists, and I will mention few like Andrew Ntshabele, Donald Kilpatrick and Solomon Omolayo Omogboye.
